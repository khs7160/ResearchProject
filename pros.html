PROS

There are many pros of using AI in the workforce. Businesses are using AI to improve the productivity of the employees. They are good for handling repetitive tasks for an organization so that employees can be used in more productive ways.

  • Can use human workforce in a more productive/impactful way
  • Efficiency (automation)
  • Reduces human error
  • Safety (physically)

There are jobs that AI are not good at. Some industry will continue to use human workers in areas such as:

  • Writing jobs
  • Social Work
  • Criminal defense law
  • Teaching jobs
  • Writers
  • Lawyers
  • Social worker
  • Therapists
  • Medical professionals
