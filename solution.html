SOLUTION

Here are some solutions I came up with to fix unemployment caused by AI.

  • Make laws around which area gets to use AI while protecting blue collar job employment.
  • Work on providing fair and equal education to population so everyone can have equal chance of getting a white collar job.

When you are making this kind of law, you would ideally want it to be international so no country's human workforce is replaced to large extent. You also should not outright ban AI from being used but set up a limit of how to use them so it doesn't interfere with the job market. We should make laws around jobs that should not use AI but require human workforce. For example, judge, management, or any job are that require ethical decisn making and human involvement should not use AI.

The law should aim both low and high income to prevent them losing jobs from AI.

We should try to provide good education to low income households so they can compete for white collar jobs since blue collar jobs will be scares with AI taking over. The use of AI is likely to create more jobs for white collar jobs while decreasing blue collar jobs which means higher education will be even more required in the future. So we should work on providing better laws for general population.
