AI and machines taking over our jobs

The introduction of AI and how advanced they are getting definitley made our lives easier. However, some of these AIs started to take over our jobs which made some people very unhappy. Since 2000, robots and automated systems slowly started to take over manufacturing jobs, 1.7 million of them. While they are taking over jobs, AI also managed to create 97 million new jobs.

This might make you think, "97 million new jobs? Isn't that great?" However you have to think about what kind of jobs AI is taking over. As of now, AIs are used mostly in blue collar jobs such as factory workers, manufacturers, truckers, mcdonald kiosks to name a few. This means receiving better education to help yourself compete for new jobs is even more pivotal.

I will walk you through how to fix this issue of AI taking over jobs while providing information of pros of cons of incorporating AI to the workplace.
